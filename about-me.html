Hi, my name is Brenden Carvalho, I am a graphic designer that takes a high interest in anything game related. I love to spend my free time creating things that revolve around what I am currently playing usually. I grew up playing sports my entire life and found a heavy interest in design once I attended Umass Dartmouth. I love everything that graphic design has to offer and the limitless possibilities that comes with graphic design. I am a determined and hard working person, I like to complete my tasks to the best of my ability every single time and I guess that is the competitive athlete in me!
